Support / Soporte

BetterMail AI is an Outlook add-in that rewrites drafts and suggests professional replies while keeping the user in control.

Install and open / Instalacion y apertura

  1. Install BetterMail AI from Outlook Apps or AppSource when available.
  2. Open a new email or reply in Outlook.
  3. Open BetterMail AI from the compose window add-in menu.

Rewrite an email / Reescribir un correo

Write a draft, choose a tone and let BetterMail AI generate a clearer version. You can replace the draft, insert the improved version below, copy it or regenerate it.

Billing / Facturacion

The trial includes limited free improvements. BetterMail Pro includes 300 improvements per 30-day billing period. Open the Pro card in Outlook to view plan status, cancel renewal at the end of the current period or reactivate it before expiration.

If a renewal payment is rejected, BetterMail displays a payment warning and provides a short grace period to update the payment method before Pro expires.

FAQ

Does BetterMail AI send emails automatically? No. You choose when to apply or send text.

Does it read my whole mailbox? No. It processes only the draft or context needed for the action you request.

Can I delete my data? Yes. Send a request to support.
