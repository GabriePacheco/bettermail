BetterMail AI certification access

Test the Outlook add-in without creating a BetterMail account. The add-in uses the Outlook profile supplied by Office.js.

Certification steps

  1. Install the submitted manifest in Outlook on the web or Outlook desktop.
  2. Open a new message, type Necesito que me envies el reporte hoy., and launch BetterMail AI.
  3. Confirm a complete professional email is generated. Change the tone and verify the current suggestion remains visible.
  4. Use Replace and confirm the Outlook signature remains intact. Also test Insert below, Copy, and Regenerate.
  5. Reply to an existing email with an empty draft and confirm BetterMail AI suggests a response using the quoted context.
  6. Activate temporary Pro access with the form on this page, then reopen the taskpane.
  7. Select My tone / Mi tono, create a custom personality, and generate another suggestion.

Expected behavior

BetterMail AI never sends an email automatically. The reviewer always chooses whether to replace, insert, or copy generated text.

Temporary certification access lasts seven days, does not charge a payment method, and does not renew automatically.
