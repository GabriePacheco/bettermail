Privacy Policy / Politica de privacidad

BetterMail AI helps users rewrite Outlook email drafts and generate professional replies. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the add-in.

BetterMail AI ayuda a reescribir borradores y generar respuestas profesionales en Outlook. Esta politica explica que datos se procesan al usar el complemento.

Data we process / Datos que procesamos

  • Your email address and display name when Outlook provides them.
  • Email draft text that you voluntarily send for rewriting.
  • Email context only when you request a suggested reply.
  • Usage metadata such as date, tone, mode, input length and output length.
  • Plan, trial and payment status for BetterMail Pro.
  • A PayPhone payment token and limited card metadata, such as brand and last digits, when needed for recurring billing. BetterMail AI does not receive or store the full card number or security code.

How we use data / Como usamos los datos

We use this data to rewrite emails, suggest replies, control trial and Pro limits, improve reliability, prevent abuse and provide support.

Service providers / Proveedores

BetterMail AI uses Google Cloud Run for backend processing, Firestore for app data, OpenAI for AI generation and PayPhone for payments when enabled.

What we do not do / Lo que no hacemos

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not store credit card numbers.
  • We do not read your entire mailbox.
  • We do not send emails automatically.

Deletion requests / Eliminacion de datos

You can request data deletion or privacy support by contacting gabriel.pacheco.developer@gmail.com.
