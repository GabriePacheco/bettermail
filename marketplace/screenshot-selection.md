## Do Not Submit Publicly Unless Anonymized

- Files ending in `-raw.png`.
- Any screenshot showing personal emails, real names, real client domains, or production conversations.

## Recommended Final Pass

- Use demo accounts such as `demo@bettermailai.com`.
- Use generic names such as `Ana Lopez` and `Carlos Rivera`.
- Avoid customer names, employer names, private domains, and real message history.
- Keep screenshots focused on one feature each.
- Make sure text is readable at normal browser zoom.
