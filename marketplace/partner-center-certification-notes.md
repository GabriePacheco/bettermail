BetterMail AI does not require a separate BetterMail account or sign-in. It uses the Outlook user profile supplied by Office.js and works immediately after installation.

INSTALLATION AND FREE-FEATURE TEST

1. Install the submitted Outlook add-in manifest.
2. In Outlook on the web or Outlook desktop, open a new message and launch BetterMail AI from the compose command surface.
3. Type: "Necesito que me envies el reporte hoy."
4. Confirm that BetterMail AI generates a complete professional email.
5. Change the selected tone and confirm the current suggestion remains visible.
6. Test Replace, Insert below, Copy, and Regenerate.
7. Add an Outlook signature, use Replace, and confirm the signature remains intact.
8. Reply to an existing email with an empty draft and confirm a response is suggested from the quoted context.
