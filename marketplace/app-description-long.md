BetterMail AI is an Outlook add-in that helps professionals improve email drafts, generate polished replies, and adjust tone without leaving Outlook.

Users can rewrite drafts, generate suggested replies from email context, and choose tones such as professional, clear, friendly, firm, and executive. BetterMail AI is designed for everyday business communication: client responses, formal requests, internal coordination, and firm but respectful messages.

The user stays in control. BetterMail AI does not send emails automatically; users choose when to replace, insert, copy, or regenerate suggested text.

BetterMail AI es un complemento de Outlook que ayuda a mejorar borradores, generar respuestas profesionales y ajustar el tono sin salir de Outlook. El usuario siempre revisa y decide cuando aplicar el texto sugerido.
